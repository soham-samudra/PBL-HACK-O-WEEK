My Travel Blog

Exploring the World, One Journey at a Time

Hello! My name is Soham Samudra and I am a Computer Science student living in Nagpur, Maharashtra. I enjoy travelling and exploring new places whenever I get time from my studies. Through this travel blog I share some of my favorite travel memories, experiences and beautiful destinations I have visited. Travelling helps me relax, learn about new cultures and enjoy the beauty of nature.

My Trip to Goa

Goa was an amazing trip with beautiful beaches and a relaxing environment. I enjoyed the sea breeze, watched sunsets and tried delicious seafood. The peaceful beaches made the trip unforgettable.

Location: Goa | June 2022

Adventure in Manali

Manali is surrounded by beautiful mountains and cool weather. I enjoyed trekking, exploring valleys and taking photos of the snowy peaks. It was one of the most adventurous trips of my life.

Location: Manali | January 2023

Exploring Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City, is full of history and culture. I visited famous forts and explored colorful local markets. The architecture and traditions made the trip very memorable.

Location: Jaipur | December 2024
